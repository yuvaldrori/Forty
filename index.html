Forty

This applications syncs podcasts listening history from PocketCasts to your calendar.

Prerequisites:

  • A writable calendar "Forty".
  • Enable notification access for the "Forty" app as described here under "Notification access".

Help needed:

  • Reading notification contentView to get book name and episode from the Audible app.
  • Settings screen to allow setting your own calnedar and to help enable the right permissions.
